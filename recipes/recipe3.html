Vegetable and Ham Noodle Cups

Description

A great way to hide vegetables

Ingredients

  • 170 g Maggi chicken-flavoured 2-minute noodles
  • 3 cups mixed vegetables grated
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup light sour cream
  • 1/2 cup ham (deli) chopped
  • 1/2 cup light tasty cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. Lightly spray a muffin pan with olive oil.
  3. Cook noodles as directed - reserving flavour sachets - and then drain.
  4. Add reserved flavour sachets and remaining ingredients, and mix well.
  5. Spoon equal portions into muffin pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until set.
