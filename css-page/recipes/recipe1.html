Pork and Stir-Fry Noodles

Description

Looks way fancier than it is

Ingredients

  • 500g diced pork
  • 1 garlic clove crushed
  • 2cm ginger chopped
  • 1 tbs curry powder
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 200g 2-minute noodles
  • 1 onion diced large
  • 1 red capsicum small sliced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup spring onions chopped

Steps

  1. Season pork with garlic, ginger and curry powder.
  2. Beat eggs and fry, like a pancake, with a little oil, remove and chop roughly.
  3. Cook noodles as directed and drain.
  4. Saute onion and capsicum in part of the oil, thaw green peas under hot water,
    drain and saute for 1 minute. Remove vegetables from pan.
  5. Stir-fry pork in remaining oil for approximately 5 minutes, add noodles and
    toss together, mix in vegetables and reheat.
  6. Add soy sauce and serve with egg and chopped shallots.
