Chicken Noodle Omelette

Description

Great omelette for kids and stuff

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 85 g chicken-flavoured 2-minute noodles
  • 1 cup chicken chopped cooked
  • 2 tsp parsley finely chopped
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • 2 tbs cheese grated

Steps

  1. Boil water in a small pot.
  2. Add noodles and flavour sachet to the pot, and once noodles are tender, drain.
  3. Combine noodles, chicken, parsley and eggs in a bowl and mix well.
  4. Pour the mixture into a 20cm non-stick frypan.
  5. Cook over medium heat until centre is just firm.
  6. Sprinkle cheese over top of omelette and put under a hot grill until cheese is
    melted and browned.
